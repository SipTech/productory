## Usage decisions

- `README.md`: `productory-logo-primary-light.png`
- `docs/quickstart.md`: `productory-logo-primary-light.png`
- `docs/api-examples.md`: `productory-logo-flat-light.png`
- Dark slide decks or dark UI shells: `productory-logo-primary-dark.png`
- Single-color surfaces (stickers, embossing, monochrome exports): `productory-logo-monochrome-dark.png`

## Rules

- Do not stretch logos; keep original aspect ratio.
- Keep at least 24px clear space around the mark.
- Prefer the primary logo variants unless contrast requires an alternate.
